About Morning Star Academy

Providing quality, affordable, and inclusive education in Northern Ghana since 2016

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Morning Star International School in Tamale is a private basic school aimed at broadening access to quality higher education in Northern Ghana. Our institution was established with a clear mission to provide affordable and inclusive education that meets the diverse needs of our community.

The school has a very conducive teaching and learning environment, designed to foster academic excellence and personal growth. In line with our mission to offer affordable and inclusive education, Morning Star International School uses curriculum-based methods and student-centered approaches that run from preschool through basic education levels.

We are proud to be approved by NASIA (National Schools Inspectorate Authority) and licensed by the Ghana Education Service, ensuring that our educational standards meet national requirements and international best practices.
